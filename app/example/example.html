Within the Payment Services department, you are part of a team of professionals that play a key role providing in-depth analysis and timely resolutions/workarounds for client issues. This position requires strong analytical, communication and problem-solving skills. Many with banking, mortgage, escrow and insurance backgrounds have found this to a rewarding career transition. 

 

Essential Duties and Responsibilities: (other duties may be assigned)

  • Execute Payroll related transaction processes in a timely and accurate manner.
  • Record transactions processed after receiving confirmation of completion
  • Research and resolve issues related to transactions
  • Assist internal and external customers with questions related to transactions
  • Adhere to Audit Controls as related to transactions

Required Qualifications/Skills:

  • Highly motivated and team oriented
  • Must be able to manage in an organized, proactive and independent manner to meet all required time frames and commitments in a high volume environment to meet all required time frames and commitments
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications: Word, and Excel
  • Extremely detail oriented with a passion for doing tasks right the first time and handling multiple priorities simultaneously
  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • Passion for customer excellence in customer service and the ability to work in a fast paced environment

Preferred Qualifications/Skills:

  • Experience working with Payroll and Tax is a plus
  • Experience with accounting and/or banking processes

Education/Certification/License:

  • BA in Business, Accounting, Computer Science preferred or a related field or equivalent work experience will be considered
